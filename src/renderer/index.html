Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How does Ticklo track my time?
Ticklo monitors your active window every second and records which application is in focus. It automatically groups continuous usage into sessions and calculates total time spent in each app.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes! All your tracking data is stored locally on your computer in the ./data folder. Ticklo never sends any data to external servers. Your privacy is our priority.
Can I export my tracking data?
Absolutely! Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management and click "Export Data" to download your tracking history as a CSV file. You can also access the raw JSON files directly in the data folder.
How do I pause tracking temporarily?
Click the pause button in the header or use the system tray menu to pause/resume tracking. When paused, no activity will be recorded until you resume.
What happens when I close the app?
By default, Ticklo minimizes to the system tray when you close the window, continuing to track in the background. You can fully quit the app from the tray menu or disable this behavior in Settings.
How far back can I view my history?
Ticklo stores data for each day in separate files, so you can view your history as far back as you've been using the app. Use the date navigation arrows to browse through past days.
Can I manually add activities?
Yes! Click the "+" button in the timeline view to manually add an activity. This is useful for tracking offline work or activities that weren't automatically captured.
